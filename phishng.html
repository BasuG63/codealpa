What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber-attack where criminals trick individuals into revealing sensitive information.

Common Types of Phishing

  • Email Phishing
  • Website Spoofing
  • Smishing (SMS Phishing)
  • Vishing (Voice Phishing)

Example of a Phishing Email

From: security@paypal-support.com

Subject: Urgent: Your Account is Suspended!

Dear User,

Your PayPal account has been restricted due to unusual activity. Click the link below to verify your account immediately:

Verify My Account

Thank you,
PayPal Security Team
